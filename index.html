Koha — ILS

Cataloging • Circulation • OPAC • Reports
Trusted ILS for handling physical collections with role-based access and detailed reporting.

Why Koha?

  • Full-featured catalog & circulation
  • Customizable OPAC UI
  • Active community & support
D

DSpace — Repository

Theses • Research • Datasets • Preservation
Long-term preservation for institutional research outputs with flexible access controls.

Why DSpace?

  • Stable repository framework
  • OAI-PMH & metadata support
  • Integration-ready APIs
